How to Use

1. Press spacebar or tap the timer to start 15s inspection.

2. To start the solve, PRESS and HOLD the spacebar or timer.

3. The timer turns GREEN after 0.55s — RELEASE to start.

4. Press ANY KEY or TAP to stop.

5. Press ESC during inspection to cancel.
